    ADMISSION TERMS & CONDITIONS

     

    • By registering for SICW you are deemed to have consented to all Terms and Conditions.

    • Registration and admission to the Singapore International Cyber Week (SICW), International Common Criteria Conference (ICCC) GovernmentWare (GovWare) is restricted to trade professionals only.

    • All attendees must be at least 18 years of age and in appropriately dressed in business attire to attend all SICW events.

    • A valid government issued photo id and business card are required for SICW pass collection.

    • All SICW-issued passes are the sole property of SICW and must be surrendered upon demand by the Organisers.

    • The Organiser reserves the right to refuse admission to anyone who does not fulfil admission requirements or to expel from the event anyone deemed to have breached the SICW Code of Conduct. 

    • All attendees are to read and understand the Data Protection and Privacy Notice which explains the collection, use and protection of personal data.

    PAYMENT, CANCELLATION & SUBSTITUTION POLICIES

     

    • Registration is not considered complete until payment(s) has been received in full and a confirmation email with receipt issued.

    • No communication from any third party other than the organisers of SICW may be deemed as confirmation of the registration including confirmation from the registrant’s bank or credit card company.

    • All Early Bird discounts require payment at the time of registration  and on/or before the cut-off date in order to be applicable.

    All requests for cancellations are subject to the following deadlines. Refunds will be less any credit card and / or admin processing fees.


    Before 26 August 2019 : 100% Refund
    Before 16 September 2019 : 50% Refund
    From 16 September 2019 : No Refunds

    • All requests for cancellations and substitutions must be submitted via email to registration@govware.sg.
