    GENERAL INFORMATION

    ADMISSION CRITERIA

    • Admission to the Singapore International Cyber Week is restricted to trade professionals only. While there is no charge to visit the GovernmentWare exhibition, the organiser reserves the right to deny entry to visitors who do not fulfil admission requirements.

    • Admission to GovernmentWare Conference is subject to registration and fees payable.

    • Admission to certain events in the SICW schedule are by invite only, and are not open for registration.

    • Minors aged 16 years and below will not be allowed entry.

    • All attendees must be in business attire. 

    • The Organiser reserves the right to refuse admission to visitors who are not appropriately dressed or do not fulfil admission requirements.

    • All participants will have to register for a pass. Passes are non-transferable due to security reasons. You may be asked by security personnel to present proof of identification before entry into the exhibition.

    • Valid Photo ID and Business Card are required for pass collection on site.

    • By registering for SICW you are deemed to have consented to all Terms and Conditions.

    • Registration and admission to the Singapore International Cyber Week (SICW) and GovernmentWare (GovWare) is restricted to trade professionals only.

    • All attendees must be at least 18 years of age and in appropriately dressed in business attire to attend all SICW events.

    • A valid government issued photo id and business card are required for SICW pass collection.

    • All SICW-issued passes are the sole property of SICW and must be surrendered upon demand by the Organisers.

    VENUE - SUNTEC SINGAPORE CONVENTION & EXHIBITION CENTRE

    • Suntec Singapore is the World's Leading Meetings & Conference Centre, located at the heart of Asia's most integrated meetings, conventions and exhibitions hub. 

    • With great versatility featuring 42,000m  of flexible customisable space, free WiFi, digital signage, an excellent range of culinary choices and a dedicated team of service experts, this award-winning facility can cater to events from 10 to 10,000 persons. 

    • Only 20 minutes from Changi International Airport, Suntec Singapore is conveniently located in the Central Business District and just minutes from the city's entertainment and cultural attractions.     GETTING THERE

    CAR

    Please park in the Green Zone of Suntec Singapore carpark, for the most direct route to the Conference Venue.

     

    Roads that lead to Suntec Singapore are:

    • Nicoll Highway

    • Raffles Boulevard (from Bras Basah Road)

    • Temasek Avenue (from Raffles Boulevard)

    • Rochor Road exit from East Coast Expressway (ECP)

    MRT

    MRT Services

    While Esplanade station and Promenade station on the Circle Line are linked directly to Suntec City Mall, walk via Raffles City and Esplanade Xchange to get from City Hall MRT station to Suntec.

    Walking Directions

    • From Promenade MRT (3 mins): Take Exit C and enter Suntec City

    • From Esplanade MRT (3 mins): Take Exit A and go up the escalator straight ahead (at the entrance of Esplanade Xchange) to Suntec City

    • From City Hall MRT (8-10 mins): Turn right after the exit gantries and walk through City Link Mall. Follow the directional signage at the end of the mall to the escalators leading to Suntec City (same exit as from Esplanade MRT)

    VISA REQUIREMENTS

    Visas are not required for citizens of the USA, EU and Commonwealth countries.

    Generally, free entry with a minimum of 14 days is the norm, though citizens of the USA may be eligible for as many as 90 free entry days. For further information, please check with your countrys Singapore Consulate, or click here to visit the Singapore Immigration & Checkpoints Authority website. 

    If you require a letter of invitation for the Visa application, please register for the conference, fill in Form V39A, and send it to registration@govware.sg.

    GOVWARE INVITATION LETTER

    If you require an invitation letter for approval from your company to attend GovernmentWare, please send an email to registration@govware.sg with the following details: Full Name, Job Title, Company, Contact Number.

    If you are a foreign attendee, kindly provide us with your passport number as well, as the invitation letter may also assist you in procuring the required visa for entry into Singapore
